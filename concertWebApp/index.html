The inspiring story

Here are some inspiring stories

In Sydney, David Brown is working hard to build firebreaks to protect communities from wildfires. Although there is still a long way to go from the target, they have received funding support of 7000.00 AUD to help them continue advancing this important project.

In Melbourne, Richard Martin's team is establishing protected areas for wildlife. They have already raised 18000.00 AUD, one step closer to their goal of 90000.00 AUD. This project will provide a safe home for local wildlife.

In Guilin, Linda Thomas is raising funds for students to donate more books. At present, we have raised 2000.00 AUD, which is only one step away from our goal of 3000.00 AUD. Every book will ignite children's thirst for knowledge.

In New York, Barbara Jackson is dedicated to restoring damaged ecosystems through tree planting. Although only 2000.00 AUD has been raised so far, her team is confident and hopes to raise 7000.00 AUD in the future.

In California, James White is raising funds for an education fund. They have raised 5500.00 AUD to help more students access better educational opportunities.
